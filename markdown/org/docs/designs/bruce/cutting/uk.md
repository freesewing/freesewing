title: "Bruce boxer briefs: Cutting Instructions"
- - -

Bruce consists of a back, and two sides, insets and fronts.

- **Main fabric**
  - Cut **1 back** on the fold
  - Cut **2 fronts**
  - Cut **2 insets**
  - Cut **2 sides**

<Warning>

###### Caveats

- **back**: Extra hem allowance at the leg
- **front**: Cut this **twice**

</Warning>
