---
title: Schulter bis Ellenbogen
---

Das **Schulter bis Ellenbogen**-Maß läuft von der Kante deiner Schulter bis zum Ellenbogen.

To measure your **shoulder to elbow**, start at the tip of your shoulder, the tip or corner of the protruding bone that is located where the top of the shoulder meets the side of the arm. It is also where the top of the shoulder seam sits and the sleeve begins on a well-fitting dress shirt. Folge deinem Arm zu deinem Ellenbogen.
