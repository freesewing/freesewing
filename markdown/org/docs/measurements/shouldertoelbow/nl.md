---
title: Schouder tot elleboog
---

De afmeting **schouder tot elleboog** loopt van de rand van je schouder tot aan je elleboog.

To measure your **shoulder to elbow**, start at the tip of your shoulder, the tip or corner of the protruding bone that is located where the top of the shoulder meets the side of the arm. It is also where the top of the shoulder seam sits and the sleeve begins on a well-fitting dress shirt. Meet langs je arm tot aan je elleboog.
