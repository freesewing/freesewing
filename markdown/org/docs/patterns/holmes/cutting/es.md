title: Cutting
---

 - **Main fabric**
   - Cut the amount of **gores** the you selected in the pattern options
   - Cut **2 ear** parts
   - Cut **4 brim** parts
 - **Lining fabric**
   - Cut the amount of **gores** the you selected in the pattern options
   - Cut **2 ear** parts
 - **Plastic**
   - Cut **2 brim**.
