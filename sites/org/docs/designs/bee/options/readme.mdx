## Fit {#fit}
### Show Bella outline {#bellaguide}

**Do you want the pattern to show the outline of the Bella block Bee is based on?**
- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Bellaguide />

### Bottom depth {#bottomcupdepth}

**Controls how far the bikini cup extends downwards**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **8%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Bottomcupdepth />

### Bust span ease {#bustspanease}

**Controls the bust span ease in the underlying Bella block Bee is based on**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **10%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Bustspanease />

### Chest ease {#chestease}

**Controls the chest ease in the underlying Bella block Bee is based on**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **11%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Chestease />

### Front curve {#frontcurve}

**Controls the curvature of the front of the bikini cup**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-50%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Frontcurve />

### Full chest reduction (Bella) {#fullchesteasereduction}

**Controls the full chest reduction in the underlying Bella block Bee is based on**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **4%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **8%**

<Fullchesteasereduction />

### Shoulder to Shoulder ease (Bella) {#shouldertoshoulderease}

**Controls the shoulder to shoulder ease in the underlying Bella block Bee is based on**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **-0.5%**
- Minimum: **-1%**
- Maximum: **5%**

<Shouldertoshoulderease />

### Side curve {#sidecurve}

**Controls the curvature of the side of the bikini cup**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-50%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Sidecurve />

### Side depth {#sidedepth}

**Controls how far the bikini cup extends towards the side**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **20.6%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **30%**

<Sidedepth />

### Top Depth {#topdepth}

**Controls how far the bikini cup extends upwards**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **54%**
- Minimum: **50%**
- Maximum: **80%**

<Topdepth />

### Waist ease {#waistease}

**Controls the waist ease in the underlying Bella block Bee is based on**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **1%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Waistease />

## Style {#style}
### Band Length (Cross back ties) {#bandlength}

**Controls the length of the band around your chest for the cross back ties variation of Bee**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **85%**
- Minimum: **75%**
- Maximum: **90%**

<Bandlength />

### Band (chest) tie length {#bandtielength}

**Controls the length of the ties around your chest**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **35%**
- Minimum: **30%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Bandtielength />

### Band (chest) tie width {#bandtiewidth}

**Controls the width of the ties around your chest**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **3%**
- Minimum: **1%**
- Maximum: **9%**

<Bandtiewidth />

### Cross back ties {#crossbackties}

- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Crossbackties />

### Duo-colored ties {#duocolorties}

- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

### Neck tie length {#necktielength}

- Maximum: **100%**

<Necktielength />

### Neck tie width {#necktiewidth}

**Controls the width of the ties around your chest**

- Minimum: **2%**
- Maximum: **18%**

<Necktiewidth />

### Pointed tie ends {#pointedtieends}

- Default: **false**

<Pointedtieends />

### Reversible {#reversible}

**Whether you want to make a fully reversible Bee or not**
- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Reversible />

- Default: **true**

<Ties />
