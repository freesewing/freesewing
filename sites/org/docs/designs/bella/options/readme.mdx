## Armhole {#armhole}
### Armhole depth {#armholedepth}

**Controls the depth of the armhole**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **44%**
- Minimum: **38%**
- Maximum: **46%**

<Armholedepth />

### Back armhole curvature {#backarmholecurvature}

**Controls how deep the armhole is scooped out at the back bottom**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **63%**
- Minimum: **50%**
- Maximum: **85%**

<Backarmholecurvature />

### Back armhole pitch depth {#backarmholepitchdepth}

**Tweaks the horizontal placement of the back armhole pitch point**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **35%**
- Minimum: **30%**
- Maximum: **40%**

<Backarmholepitchdepth />

### Back armhole slant {#backarmholeslant}

**Slightly rotates the armhole around its pitch point**
- Type: **Degrees**
- Default: **5°**
- Minimum: **1°**
- Maximum: **9°**

<Backarmholeslant />

### Front armhole curvature {#frontarmholecurvature}

**Controls how deep the armhole is scooped out at the front bottom**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **63%**
- Minimum: **50%**
- Maximum: **85%**

<Frontarmholecurvature />

### Front armhole pitch depth {#frontarmholepitchdepth}

**Tweaks the horizontal placement of the front armhole pitch point**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **29%**
- Minimum: **27%**
- Maximum: **31%**

<Frontarmholepitchdepth />

## Darts {#darts}
### Back dart height {#backdartheight}

**Controls the height of the back dart**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **46%**
- Minimum: **38%**
- Maximum: **54%**

<Backdartheight />

### Bust dart angle {#bustdartangle}

**The angle of the bust dart**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **undefined**
- Minimum: **-45**
- Maximum: **45**

<Bustdartangle />

### Bust dart curve {#bustdartcurve}

**Controls the curvature of the bust dart**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **100%**
- Minimum: **-100%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Bustdartcurve />

### Bust dart length {#bustdartlength}

**Controls the length of the bust dart**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **90%**
- Minimum: **75%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Bustdartlength />

### Bust dart minimum fabric {#bustdartminimumfabric}

**The minimum amount of side seam above and below the bust dart**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **1%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Bustdartminimumfabric />

### Waist dart curve {#waistdartcurve}

**Controls the curvature of the waist dart**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **100%**
- Minimum: **-100%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Waistdartcurve />

### Waist dart length {#waistdartlength}

**Controls the length of the waist dart**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **90%**
- Minimum: **75%**
- Maximum: **95%**

<Waistdartlength />

## Fit {#fit}
### Bust span ease {#bustspanease}

**Controls the amount of (horizontal) ease added to your bust span when locating the bust point.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **10%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Bustspanease />

### Chest ease {#chestease}

**Controls the amount of ease at the fullest part of your chest**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **11%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Chestease />

### Full chest ease reduction {#fullchesteasereduction}

**Allows you to independently reduce the ease around the chest to make it fit tight(er) in that area**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **4%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **8%**

<Fullchesteasereduction />

### Shoulder to Shoulder ease {#shouldertoshoulderease}

**Controls the amount of ease between your shoulders. Initially set to -.5% because Bella implements a block that is used in the industry.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **-0.5%**
- Minimum: **-1%**
- Maximum: **5%**

<Shouldertoshoulderease />

### Waist ease {#waistease}

**Controls the amount of ease at your waist**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **1%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Waistease />

## Advanced {#advanced}
### Back hem slope {#backhemslope}

**Controls the slope of the hem at the back**
- Type: **Degrees**
- Default: **2.5°**
- Minimum: **0°**
- Maximum: **5°**

<Backhemslope />

### Back neck cutout {#backneckcutout}

**Controls how deep the neck opening is scooped out at at the back**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **6%**
- Minimum: **3%**
- Maximum: **9%**

<Backneckcutout />

### Front shoulder width {#frontshoulderwidth}

**Controls the narrowness of the front shoulders relative to the back**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **95%**
- Minimum: **92%**
- Maximum: **98%**

<Frontshoulderwidth />

### High bust width {#highbustwidth}

**Allows you to tweak the hight bust width at the front**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **86%**
- Minimum: **80%**
- Maximum: **92%**

<Highbustwidth />
