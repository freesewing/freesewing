## Fit {#fit}
### Knee ease {#kneeease}

**Controls the amount of ease at the knee**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **15%**
- Minimum: **10%**
- Maximum: **30%**

<Kneeease />

### Seat ease {#seatease}

**Controls the amount of ease at your seat**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **10%**

<Seatease />

### Waist ease {#waistease}

**Controls the amount of ease at your waist**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **1%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **5%**

<Waistease />

### Waistband Curve {#waistbandcurve}

**Controls how curved the waistband is.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **35%**

<Waistbandcurve />

## Style {#style}
### Crotch drop {#crotchdrop}

**Lowers the crotch for a more relaxed fit**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **2%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **15%**

<Crotchdrop />

### Length bonus {#lengthbonus}

**Controls the length of the trousers**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **2%**
- Minimum: **-20%**
- Maximum: **10%**

<Lengthbonus />

### Use waistband angle for {#usewaistanglefor}

**Apply the waistband angle to the front, back or both**
- Type: **List**
- Default: **both**
- options:
  - both
  - backOnly
  - frontOnly

<Usewaistanglefor />

### Waistband angle {#waistangle}

**Change the angle of the waistband when viewed from the side**
- Type: **Degrees**
- Default: **0°**
- Minimum: **-20°**
- Maximum: **20°**

<Waistangle />

### Waist height {#waistheight}

**Controls the height of the waist, 100% = waist height, 0% = hip height**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **-4%**
- Minimum: **-15%**
- Maximum: **40%**

<Waistheight />

### Waistband width {#waistbandwidth}

**The width of the waistband**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **3%**
- Minimum: **1%**
- Maximum: **6%**

<Waistbandwidth />
