## Conditional Options {#conditional}
### Armhole depth {#armholedepth}

**Controls the depth of the armhole. Higher values make a deeper armhole.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **2%**
- Minimum: **-10%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Armholedepth />

### Armhole depth factor (legacy) {#armholedepthfactor}

**Controls the depth of the armhole. Higher values make a deeper armhole. This option only applies to the legacy (v2) way of calculating the armhole depth.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **55%**
- Minimum: **50%**
- Maximum: **70%**

<Armholedepthfactor />

### Draft for high bust {#draftforhighbust}

**Draft the pattern using the high bust measurement if it is available, instead of using the chest measurement. This can result in a more fitted garment for people with breasts. (Please note that no additional adjustments or shaping is performed. Further manual adjustments may be needed for those with a larger difference between high bust and chest measurements.)**
- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Draftforhighbust />

## Fit {#fit}
### Biceps ease {#bicepsease}

**The amount of ease at your upper arm. Note that while we try to respect this, fitting the sleeve to the armhole takes precedence over respecting the exact amount of ease.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **15%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Bicepsease />

### Chest ease {#chestease}

**The amount of ease at your chest.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **15%**
- Minimum: **-4%**
- Maximum: **35%**

<Chestease />

### Collar ease {#collarease}

**The amount of ease around your neck**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **10%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **30%**

<Collarease />

### Cuff ease {#cuffease}

**The amount of ease at your wrist.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **20%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **200%**

<Cuffease />

### Hips ease {#hipsease}

**Controls the amount of ease at your hips (the bottom of the sweater)**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **8%**
- Minimum: **-4%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Hipsease />

### Ribbing stretch {#ribbingstretch}

**The amount of negative ease to apply to the ribbing used for cuffs and hem.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **15%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **30%**

<Ribbingstretch />

### Shoulder ease {#shoulderease}

**The amount of ease at your shoulder. This increases the shoulder to shoulder distance to accommodate additional layers or thickness.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-2%**
- Maximum: **6%**

<Shoulderease />

## Style {#style}
### Length bonus {#lengthbonus}

**The amount to lengthen the garment. A negative value will shorten it.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **15%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **30%**

<Lengthbonus />

### Ribbing {#ribbing}

**Whether to finish the hem and cuffs with ribbing or not.**
- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **true**

<Ribbing />

### Ribbing height {#ribbingheight}

**The height of the ribbing on cuffs and hem.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **8%**
- Minimum: **3%**
- Maximum: **15%**

<Ribbingheight />

### Shoulder seam shift: armhole side {#s3armhole}

**Increase this option to shift the shoulder seam forward on the armhole side. Decreasing it shifts it backwards.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-100%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<S3armhole />

### Shoulder seam shift: collar side {#s3collar}

**Increase this option to shift the shoulder seam forward on the collar side. Decreasing it shifts it backwards.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-100%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<S3collar />

### Sleeve length bonus {#sleevelengthbonus}

**The amount to lengthen the sleeve. A negative value will shorten it.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **3%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **10%**

<Sleevelengthbonus />

## Advanced {#advanced}
### Across back factor {#acrossbackfactor}

**Controls your across back width as a factor of your shoulder to shoulder measurement.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **98%**
- Minimum: **93%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Acrossbackfactor />

### Back neck cutout {#backneckcutout}

**How deep the neck is cut out at the back**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **2%**
- Maximum: **8%**

<Backneckcutout />

### Front armhole extra cutout {#frontarmholedeeper}

**How much do you want the front armhole to be cut out deeper than the back.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0.2%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **0.5%**

<Frontarmholedeeper />

### Legacy armhole depth {#legacyarmholedepth}

**Enable this option to use the legacy way to calculate the armhole depth (using biceps circumference) rather than the v3 way (using the waist to armpit measurement)**
- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Legacyarmholedepth />

### Shoulder slope reduction {#shoulderslopereduction}

**The amount by which the shoulder slope is reduced to allow for shoulder padding.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **80%**

<Shoulderslopereduction />

### Sleeve width guarantee {#sleevewidthguarantee}

**Controls how much of the sleeve width will be guaranteed. This determines how much we can alter the sleeve width to fit the sleeve in the armhole.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **90%**
- Minimum: **25%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Sleevewidthguarantee />
