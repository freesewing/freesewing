## Conditional Options {#conditional}
### Shoulder dart length {#shoulderdartlength}

**The length of the shoulder dart**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **85%**
- Minimum: **60%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Shoulderdartlength />

### Shoulder dart size {#shoulderdartsize}

**The size of the shoulder dart**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **7%**
- Minimum: **4%**
- Maximum: **10%**

<Shoulderdartsize />

### Waist dart length {#waistdartlength}

**The length of the waist dart**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **85%**
- Minimum: **60%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Waistdartlength />

### Waist dart size {#waistdartsize}

**The size of the waist dart**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **10%**
- Minimum: **4%**
- Maximum: **15%**

<Waistdartsize />

## Fit {#fit}
### Biceps ease {#bicepsease}

**The amount of ease at your upper arm. Note that while we try to respect this, fitting the sleeve to the armhole takes precedence over respecting the exact amount of ease.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **15%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Bicepsease />

### Chest ease {#chestease}

**The amount of ease at your chest.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **10%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Chestease />

### Collar ease {#collarease}

**The amount of ease around your neck**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **3.5%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **10%**

<Collarease />

### Cuff ease {#cuffease}

**The amount of ease at your wrist.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **20%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Cuffease />

### Front scye dart {#frontscyedart}

**Controls the dart at the front of the arm opening. This can help control gaping at the armhole.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **25%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **45%**

<Frontscyedart />

### Shoulder dart {#shoulderdart}

**Whether or not to include a dart at the shoulder to round the back**
- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Shoulderdart />

### Shoulder ease {#shoulderease}

**The amount of ease at your shoulder. This increases the shoulder to shoulder distance to accommodate additional layers or thickness.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **4%**

<Shoulderease />

### Vertical ease {#verticalease}

**The amount of ease to distribute along the length of the garment**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **2%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **8%**

<Verticalease />

### Waist dart {#waistdart}

**Whether or not to include a dart at the waist to round the back**
- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **true**

<Waistdart />

### Waist ease {#waistease}

**The amount of ease at the waist**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **10%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Waistease />

- Maximum: **95%**

- Maximum: **75%**

<Primarybustdartshaping />

<Sleevelengthbonus />

## Advanced {#advanced}
### Across back factor {#acrossbackfactor}

**Controls your across back width as a factor of your shoulder to shoulder measurement.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **96%**
- Minimum: **93%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Acrossbackfactor />

### Armhole depth factor {#armholedepthfactor}

**Controls the depth of the armhole. Higher values make a deeper armhole.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **100%**
- Minimum: **80%**
- Maximum: **120%**

<Armholedepthfactor />

### Back neck cutout {#backneckcutout}

**How deep the neck is cut out at the back**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **2%**
- Maximum: **8%**

<Backneckcutout />

### Front armhole extra cutout {#frontarmholedeeper}

**How much do you want the front armhole to be cut out deeper than the back.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **1%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **5%**

<Frontarmholedeeper />

### Shoulder slope reduction {#shoulderslopereduction}

**The amount by which the shoulder slope is reduced to allow for shoulder padding.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Shoulderslopereduction />
