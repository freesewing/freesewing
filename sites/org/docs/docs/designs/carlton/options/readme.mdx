## Conditional Options {#conditional}
### Armhole depth {#armholedepth}

**Controls the depth of the armhole. Higher values make a deeper armhole.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **-10%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Armholedepth />

## Collar {#collar}
### Collar flare {#collarflare}

**Controls the flare of the collar by altering the fall length (between the neck and the tips) of the collar.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **20%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **40%**

<Collarflare />

### Collar height {#collarheight}

**Height of the collar**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **9.6%**
- Minimum: **8%**
- Maximum: **11%**

<Collarheight />

### Collar spread {#collarspread}

**Controls how close the collar lies to the neck.**
- Type: **Degrees**
- Default: **4°**
- Minimum: **2°**
- Maximum: **6°**

<Collarspread />

## Fit {#fit}
### Across back factor {#acrossbackfactor}

**Controls your across back width as a factor of your shoulder to shoulder measurement.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **97%**
- Minimum: **93%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Acrossbackfactor />

### Armhole depth factor (legacy) {#armholedepthfactor}

**Controls the depth of the armhole. Higher values make a deeper armhole. This option only applies to the legacy (v2) way of calculating the armhole depth.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **65%**
- Minimum: **50%**
- Maximum: **70%**

<Armholedepthfactor />

### Biceps ease {#bicepsease}

**The amount of ease at your upper arm. Note that while we try to respect this, fitting the sleeve to the armhole takes precedence over respecting the exact amount of ease.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **20%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Bicepsease />

### Chest ease {#chestease}

**The amount of ease at your chest.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **10%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Chestease />

### Cuff ease {#cuffease}

**The amount of ease at your wrist.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **60%**
- Minimum: **30%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Cuffease />

### Draft for high bust {#draftforhighbust}

**Draft the pattern using the high bust measurement if it is available, instead of using the chest measurement. This can result in a more fitted garment for people with breasts. (Please note that no additional adjustments or shaping is performed. Further manual adjustments may be needed for those with a larger difference between high bust and chest measurements.)**
- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Draftforhighbust />

### Seat ease {#seatease}

**Amount of ease around your bum**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **14%**
- Minimum: **8%**
- Maximum: **25%**

<Seatease />

### Shoulder ease {#shoulderease}

**The amount of ease at your shoulder. This increases the shoulder to shoulder distance to accommodate additional layers or thickness.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-2%**
- Maximum: **6%**

<Shoulderease />

### Sleeve bend {#sleevebend}

**The angle of the sleeve at the elbow.**
- Type: **Degrees**
- Default: **10°**
- Minimum: **0°**
- Maximum: **20°**

<Sleevebend />

### Sleeve length bonus {#sleevelengthbonus}

**The amount to lengthen the sleeve. A negative value will shorten it.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **7%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Sleevelengthbonus />

### Waist ease {#waistease}

**Controls the amount of ease at your waist.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **14%**
- Minimum: **8%**
- Maximum: **25%**

<Waistease />

<Beltwidth />

<Buttonspacinghorizontal />

### Cuff length {#cufflength}

<Cufflength />

- Type: **Percentage**

- Maximum: **100%**

<Length />

### Shoulder seam shift: armhole side {#s3armhole}

**Increase this option to shift the shoulder seam forward on the armhole side. Decreasing it shifts it backwards.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-100%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<S3armhole />

### Shoulder seam shift: collar side {#s3collar}

**Increase this option to shift the shoulder seam forward on the collar side. Decreasing it shifts it backwards.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-100%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<S3collar />

## Advanced {#advanced}
### Back neck cutout {#backneckcutout}

**How deep the neck is cut out at the back**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **2%**
- Maximum: **8%**

<Backneckcutout />

### Front armhole extra cutout {#frontarmholedeeper}

**How much do you want the front armhole to be cut out deeper than the back.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0.5%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **1.5%**

<Frontarmholedeeper />

### Front overlap {#frontoverlap}

**Controls how much the right and left front of the garment overlap at the center front.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **1.5%**
- Minimum: **1%**
- Maximum: **2%**

<Frontoverlap />

### Lapel reduction {#lapelreduction}

**Controls how much the lapel tip goes inwards.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **10%**

<Lapelreduction />

### Legacy armhole depth {#legacyarmholedepth}

**Enable this option to use the legacy way to calculate the armhole depth (using biceps circumference) rather than the v3 way (using the waist to armpit measurement)**
- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Legacyarmholedepth />

### Shoulder slope reduction {#shoulderslopereduction}

**The amount by which the shoulder slope is reduced to allow for shoulder padding.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **12%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **80%**

<Shoulderslopereduction />

### Sleevecap ease {#sleevecapease}

**Controls the amount of sleevecap ease.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **1%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **10%**

<Sleevecapease />

### Sleevecap height {#sleevecapheight}

**Controls the height of the sleevecap.**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **45%**

<Sleevecapheight />
