## Construction {#construction}

### Bib placket layers {#bibplacketlayers}

**Sets how many layers of fabric are used for the placket at the top of the front bib.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **undefined**
- Minimum: **0**
- Maximum: **8**

<Bibplacketlayers />

### Bib placket width {#bibplacketwidth}

**Controls how wide to make the placket at the top of the front bib.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **15%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **30%**

<Bibplacketwidth />

### Hem allowance {#hemallowance}

**Sets how wide the hems around the front and back bibs and the straps are, as a percent of the seam allowance.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **150%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **400%**

<Hemallowance />

### Waist drop {#bibwaistdrop}

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **15%**

<Bibwaistdrop />

**Controls vertical ease at the crotch fork.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **-10%**
- Maximum: **80%**

<Crotchdrop />

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **10%**
- Minimum: **-30%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Crotchease />

- Default: **10%**

- Maximum: **40%**

<Crotchforkbalance />

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-30%**
- Maximum: **30%**

<Legbalance />

- Default: **20%**

<Leghemease />

- Maximum: **15%**
