## Conditional Options {#conditional}
### Buttons {#buttons}

**Controls the number of buttons in the fly**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **undefined**
- Minimum: **1**
- Maximum: **5**

<Buttons />

### Front pocket facing {#pocketfacing}

**Enables facing the front pocket**
- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **true**

<Pocketfacing />

### Front pocket entry facing {#pockettrim}

**Enables facing the front pocket entry**
- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **true**

<Pockettrim />

### Pocket curve shape {#pocketcurveshape}

**Controls the curve of the front pocket opening**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **15%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Pocketcurveshape />

### Pocket depth {#pocketdepth}

**Controls the amount of room in your pockets**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **70%**
- Minimum: **25%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Pocketdepth />

### Pocket opening height {#pocketopeningheight}

**How high the opening of the front pockets should be**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **8%**
- Minimum: **4%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Pocketopeningheight />

### Pocket opening width {#pocketopeningwidth}

**How wide the opening of the front pockets should be**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **80%**
- Minimum: **40%**
- Maximum: **90%**

<Pocketopeningwidth />

### Gusset balance {#gussetbalance}

**Controls how much of the front part is cut off to construct the gusset**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **250%**
- Minimum: **100%**
- Maximum: **400%**

<Gussetbalance />

### Gusset size {#gussetsize}

**Controls the size of the gusset**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **40%**
- Minimum: **10%**
- Maximum: **60%**

<Gussetsize />

### Gusset width {#gussetwidth}

**Controls the width of the gusset compared to its general size**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **25%**
- Minimum: **10%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Gussetwidth />

## Fit {#fit}
### Knee ease {#kneeease}

**Controls the amount of ease at the knee**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **15%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **30%**

<Kneeease />

### Seat ease {#seatease}

**Controls the amount of ease at your seat**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **2%**
- Minimum: **-10%**
- Maximum: **10%**

<Seatease />

### Waist ease {#waistease}

**Controls the amount of ease at your waist**
- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **1%**
- Minimum: **-5%**
- Maximum: **5%**

<Waistease />

- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **15%**

<Waistbandcurve />

- Maximum: **100%**

<Ankleease />

- Maximum: **100%**

<Ankleshape />

- Maximum: **15%**

<Crotchdrop />

<Length />

<Usewaistanglefor />
