## Conditional Options {#conditional}

### Waist ease {#waistease}

**Ease at the waist (only applies when the waist is fitted).**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **25%**
- Minimum: **8%**
- Maximum: **40%**

<Waistease />

### Armhole depth factor (legacy) {#armholedepthfactor}

**Controls the depth of the armhole. Higher values make a deeper armhole. This option only applies to the legacy (v2) way of calculating the armhole depth.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **55%**
- Minimum: **50%**
- Maximum: **70%**

<Armholedepthfactor />

### Armhole depth {#armholedepth}

**Controls the depth of the armhole. Higher values make a deeper armhole.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **2%**
- Minimum: **-10%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Armholedepth />

## Fit {#fit}

### Back neck cutout {#backneckcutout}

**How deep the neck is cut out at the back**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **8%**
- Minimum: **4%**
- Maximum: **12%**

<Backneckcutout />

### Chest ease {#chestease}

**The amount of ease at your chest.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **12%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **25%**

<Chestease />

### Draft for high bust {#draftforhighbust}

**Draft the pattern for the high bust measurement (if available) rather than the (full) chest. This will result in a more fitted garment for people with breasts.**

- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Draftforhighbust />

### Fit the waist {#fitwaist}

**Whether or not to fit the waist or rather only fit chest and hips.**

- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Fitwaist />

### Hips ease {#hipsease}

**Ease at the hips.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **18%**
- Minimum: **8%**
- Maximum: **30%**

<Hipsease />

### Sleeve length {#sleevelength}

**Controls the length of your sleeves**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **30%**
- Minimum: **20%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Sleevelength />

## Style {#style}

### Length bonus {#lengthbonus}

**The amount to lengthen the garment. A negative value will shorten it.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **15%**
- Minimum: **-20%**
- Maximum: **60%**

<Lengthbonus />

### Neckline curvature {#necklinebend}

**Controls the curvature of the neckline.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **30%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **70%**

<Necklinebend />

### Neckline depth {#necklinedepth}

**Controls how deep the neck opening plunges down.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **25%**
- Minimum: **20%**
- Maximum: **40%**

<Necklinedepth />

### Neckline width {#necklinewidth}

**Controls the width of the neck opening.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **30%**
- Minimum: **10%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Necklinewidth />

### Sleeve ease {#sleeveease}

**Amount of ease of your sleeves**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **15%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **35%**

<Sleeveease />

## Advanced {#advanced}

### Across back factor {#acrossbackfactor}

**Controls your across back width as a factor of your shoulder to shoulder measurement.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **98%**
- Minimum: **93%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Acrossbackfactor />

### Legacy armhole depth {#legacyarmholedepth}

**Enable this option to use the legacy way to calculate the armhole depth (using biceps circumference) rather than the v3 way (using the waist to armpit measurement)**

- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Legacyarmholedepth />

### Sleevecap {#sleevecap}

#### Sleevecap back X {#sleevecapbackfactorx}

**Controls the placement of the sleevecap back pitchpoint on the X-axis (horizontal)**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **60%**
- Minimum: **35%**
- Maximum: **65%**

<Sleevecapbackfactorx />

#### Sleevecap back Y {#sleevecapbackfactory}

**Controls the placement of the sleevecap back pitchpoint on the Y-axis (vertical)**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **33%**
- Minimum: **30%**
- Maximum: **65%**

<Sleevecapbackfactory />

#### Sleevecap ease {#sleevecapease}

**The amount by which the sleevecap seam is longer than the armhole seam.**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **10%**

<Sleevecapease />

#### Sleevecap front X {#sleevecapfrontfactorx}

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **55%**
- Minimum: **35%**
- Maximum: **65%**

<Sleevecapfrontfactorx />

- Minimum: **30%**

<Sleevecapfrontfactory />
