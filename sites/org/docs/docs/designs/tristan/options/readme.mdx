## Conditional Options {#conditional}

### Size of the hem {#hemsize}

**Size of the hem, a percentage of the distance between the neckline and the waist**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5.62%**
- Minimum: **1%**
- Maximum: **25%**

<Hemsize />

### Shoulder dart curvature {#shoulderdartcurvature}

**Controls the curvature of the upper part of the shoulder dart**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-5%**
- Maximum: **2.5%**

<Shoulderdartcurvature />

### Location of the lacing {#lacinglocation}

**Lacing in the front, or the back**

- Type: **List**
- Default: **front**
- options:
  - front
  - back

<Lacinglocation />

### Lacing width {#lacingwidth}

**Width of the lacing opening**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **20%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **50%**

<Lacingwidth />

### Peplum fullness {#peplumfullness}

**Controls the 'fullness' of the peplum. Basically from a half-circle to a full-circle.**

- Type: **Degrees**
- Default: **180°**
- Minimum: **180°**
- Maximum: **360°**

<Peplumfullness />

### Peplum size {#peplumsize}

**Controls the size of the peplum**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **10%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **250%**

<Peplumsize />

### Hem option {#hem}

**Add a hem.**

- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Hem />

## Darts {#darts}

### Waist dart position {#waistdartposition}

**Controls the position of the waist 'dart', moving it to the center (negative) or side (positive)**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-100%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Waistdartposition />

## Fit {#fit}

### Bust span ease {#bustspanease}

**Controls the amount of ease along the bust span**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **0%**
- Minimum: **-5%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Bustspanease />

### Chest ease {#chestease}

**Controls the amount of ease at the chest**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **11%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Chestease />

### Full chest ease reduction {#fullchesteasereduction}

**Allows you to independently reduce the ease around the chest to make it fit tight(er) in that area**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **4%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **8%**

<Fullchesteasereduction />

### Shoulder to shoulder ease {#shouldertoshoulderease}

**Controls the amount of ease along the shoulder to shoulder measurement**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **-0.5%**
- Minimum: **-1%**
- Maximum: **5%**

<Shouldertoshoulderease />

### Waist ease {#waistease}

**Controls the amount of ease at the waist**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **5%**
- Minimum: **1%**
- Maximum: **20%**

<Waistease />

## options {#options}

### Lacing option {#lacing}

**This removes part of the seam to create an opening for lacing**

- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **true**

<Lacing />

### Peplum {#peplum}

**Add a peplum**

- Type: **Boolean**
- Default: **false**

<Peplum />

### Zipper location {#zipperlocation}

**The location of the zipper: side, back, or front**

- Type: **List**
- Default: **side**
- options:
  - front
  - side
  - back

<Zipperlocation />

## Style {#style}

### Depth of the Armhole {#armholedepth}

**Controls how deep the armhole is**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **44%**
- Minimum: **38%**
- Maximum: **55%**

<Armholedepth />

### Depth of the cut in the back {#cutdepthback}

**Controls the depth of the cut in the back**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **40%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **90%**

<Cutdepthback />

### Depth of the cut in the front {#cutdepthfront}

**Controls the depth of the cut in the front**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **80%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **125%**

<Cutdepthfront />

### Roundness of the cut in the back {#cutroundnessback}

**Controls how 'round' the cut in the back is, from V to round to square**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **20%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Cutroundnessback />

### Roundness of the cut in the front {#cutroundnessfront}

**Controls how 'round' the cut in the front is, from V to round to square**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **10%**
- Minimum: **0%**
- Maximum: **100%**

<Cutroundnessfront />

### Shoulder dart position {#shoulderdartposition}

**Controls the position of the shoulder dart**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **50%**
- Minimum: **10%**
- Maximum: **90%**

<Shoulderdartposition />

### Width of the straps {#strapwidth}

**Controls the width of the straps**

- Type: **Percentage**
- Default: **45%**
- Minimum: **5%**
- Maximum: **90%**

<Strapwidth />
