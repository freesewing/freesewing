Cutting is the act of cutting out the different pattern pieces out of your fabric.

Cutting is typically done with scissors or a rotary cutter.

![Joost cutting out some T-shirts](cutting.jpg 'Joost cutting out some T-shirts')

:::note
The **cut of a garment**, the way it looks, refers to how it was cut out. For
example, high-cut jeans and a low-cut top.
:::
